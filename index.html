Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about life insurance.

How much life insurance do I need?

The amount of coverage you need depends on several factors, including your income, debts, family size, and future expenses like college tuition. I can help you calculate the right amount during our consultation.

What types of life insurance do you offer?

We offer term life, whole life, and potentially universal life insurance policies (confirm specific offerings). Each has different benefits and costs. We'll help you understand which type is best for your specific situation.

How long does the application process take?

Many policies can be approved quickly, sometimes within 24-48 hours. Some policies may require a medical exam, which can extend the process. We offer expedited options for those who need coverage fast.

Is life insurance expensive?

Life insurance is often more affordable than people expect. For example, a healthy 35-year-old might pay less than $30 per month for a $500,000 term life policy. We work with multiple carriers to find you the best rates.
